##### Feline Health Issues
Regular grooming is essential to maintain a healthy coat. Nail care and dental care are crucial to prevent overgrowth and maintain good oral health. Skin problems, fleas, and ticks can cause irritation and affect a cat's appearance.

*Source: Scout (General Web Search)*

#### Dietary Recommendations
A balanced diet with essential nutrients, including protein, vitamins, and minerals, is crucial for maintaining feline health. Feeding a diet high in carbohydrates can lead to obesity and related health issues.

*Source: Librarian (TANGLE Wiki Integration)*

### Recommended Actions

1. Schedule regular veterinary check-ups.
2. Provide dental care through brushing her teeth daily or using dental chews.
3. Maintain good hygiene practices, such as regular grooming and cleaning the living environment.
